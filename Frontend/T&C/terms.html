User Agreement

Sharing User Details with Patients in Need of Blood By using our website and registering as a blood donor, you agree to the following:

  • Voluntary Consent:

    You voluntarily consent to share your contact details and blood donation information with potential patients in need of blood. This information may include your name, phone number, blood group, and location (city, state).

  • Purpose of Sharing:

    The purpose of sharing your details is to facilitate communication between potential patients and registered blood donors, enabling them to connect and coordinate blood donation.

  • Privacy and Confidentiality:

    We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. We will take reasonable measures to secure your data and only disclose it to potential patients or authorized medical professionals involved in coordinating blood donations.

  • Consent Withdrawal:

    You have the right to withdraw your consent for sharing your details at any time. If you choose to withdraw your consent, your information will no longer be accessible to potential patients seeking blood donors.

  • User Responsibility:

    As a registered blood donor, you acknowledge that you are responsible for providing accurate and up-to-date information. You understand that sharing incorrect or misleading information may result in ineffective communication and hinder the process of coordinating blood donations.

  • User Interaction:

    Any communication or interaction between users seeking blood donors and registered blood donors is solely their responsibility. We do not endorse or assume any liability for the accuracy, quality, or outcome of such interactions.

  • Indemnification:

    You agree to indemnify and hold us harmless from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising out of the sharing of your details or any disputes or interactions between users seeking blood donors and registered blood donors.
